About Tiny Wins Daily

This site doesn't track you. No analytics, no cookies, no data collection. What you do here stays here.

It doesn't judge you. Every action counts. Some days you'll do one. Some days you'll skip it. Both are okay.

It doesn't care if you miss a day. There are no streaks. No guilt. No "you were on a 5-day streak and broke it." Come back when you need to.

Your worth isn't measured by this site. You are enough without doing anything at all.

This is a quiet space for you. Use it gently. Use it when it helps. That's all it's here for.
